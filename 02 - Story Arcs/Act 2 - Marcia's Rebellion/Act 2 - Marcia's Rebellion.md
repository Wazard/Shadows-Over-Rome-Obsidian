# Act 2 - Marcia's Rebellion

Act 2 is the rebellion act. It grows from the anger seeded during [[Act 1 - Varro's Merchant Conspiracy]].

[[Marcia]] represents genuine public rage against hunger, humiliation, biased institutions, and merchant control. She is not secretly Varro's puppet, but Varro's crisis creates the conditions that make her movement possible.

Reference materials exist in `Wazard/Shadows-over-Rome/phase 2`, but this vault pass is focused on Act 1.
