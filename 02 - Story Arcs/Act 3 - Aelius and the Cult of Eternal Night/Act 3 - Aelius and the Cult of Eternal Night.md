# Act 3 - Aelius and the Cult of Eternal Night

Act 3 reveals the deepest threat: [[Aelius Nuurglaag]] and the [[Cult of Eternal Night]].

This danger is seeded quietly during Act 1 and Act 2 through sea imagery, strange symbols, black cloth, missing people, water, candles, whispers, and [[Diego]]'s increasingly frantic warnings.

## Act 3 Migration Status

Reference materials exist in `Wazard/Shadows-over-Rome/phase 3`, but this vault pass is focused on Act 1.
