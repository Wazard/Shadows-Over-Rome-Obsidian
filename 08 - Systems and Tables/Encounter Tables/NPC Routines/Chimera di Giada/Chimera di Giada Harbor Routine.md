# Chimera di Giada Harbor Routine

## Links

- Location: [[Harbor District]]
- Ship crew: [[Crew of the Chimera di Giada]]
- Harbor contact: [[Sajan Qadir]]
- Captain: [[Captain Linh Sura]]
- Cargo-master: [[Bao Yiren]]

## Default Routine

| Time | Routine |
| --- | --- |
| Morning | [[Sajan Qadir]] is at the harbor counting incoming inquiries, watching cargo movement, and taking small commissions. |
| Midday | [[Bao Yiren]] checks cargo tallies aboard or near the bonded warehouse. He refuses casual access to ledgers. |
| Afternoon | Sailors move between the ship, spice crates, gem chests, gunpowder jars, and weapon bundles under Sajan's supervision. |
| Evening | [[Captain Linh Sura]] receives serious visitors by appointment. Sajan remains the public contact. |
| Night | One armed sailor and one ledger assistant remain near the gangplank. They do not chase thieves far from the ship. |

## Commission Hooks

- Recover a crate misdirected by Roman warehouse staff.
- Escort a sealed spice chest through festival crowds.
- Find who tried to mark gunpowder jars as lamp oil.
- Identify a buyer asking about side-effect scrolls.
- Protect Sajan during a discreet meeting with a black market broker.

## Information They Can Sell Or Trade

- Which cargo was actually unloaded from *La Chimera di Giada*.
- Which Roman warehouse workers counted the crates.
- Whether a seal or manifest mark is authentic.
- Who asked about spices, gunpowder, gems, eastern weapons, or scrolls.
- A short-term rumor about suspicious dock movement.

## Boundaries

The crew does not know Varro's plan at the start of Act 1. They can point to wrong movement, false paperwork, suspicious Roman intermediaries, and cargo pressure, but they cannot prove the noble patron.
