# Campaign Endings Shadows Over Rome

Source file: `Campaign_Endings_Shadows_Over_Rome.docx`

Shadows Over Rome – Endings & Epilogues

These endings depend on player actions across the campaign.Evaluate outcomes BEFORE narrating.Do not tell players the criteria—only the consequences.

ENDING CONDITIONS (DM REFERENCE)

Track these 3 variables:1. MERCHANT POWER (Varro)- STOPPED (exposed / removed)- PARTIAL- FULL CONTROL2. REBELLION (Marcia)- RESOLVED (peace or victory)- ONGOING- COLLAPSED3. CULT PROGRESS (Aelius)- STOPPED (before Day 26)- PARTIAL (interrupted late)- COMPLETE (Day 30)FINAL ENDING = combination of these

GOOD ENDING – 'THE CITY BREATHES AGAIN'

TRIGGER:- Merchant corruption STOPPED- Rebellion RESOLVED- Cult defeated BEFORE Day 26RESULT:Rome stabilizes.The siege never fully materializes or collapses quickly.Food returns to circulation.The people slowly regain trust.The sea returns to normal.DIEGO:Never returns—but is remembered.Optional:Players find one final note:"Ve l’avevo detto."TONE:Bittersweet victoryPlayers feel:"We paid attention—and it mattered"FINAL IMAGE:The sea moves again.

MIXED ENDING – 'A CITY SCARRED'

TRIGGER:- One or two threats resolved- One left partially activeEXAMPLES:1) Merchant stopped, cult delayed, rebellion unresolved→ Political instability remains2) Rebellion wins, merchant gone, cult partially stopped→ People free, but something still wrongRESULT:Rome survives—but changed.- Distrust remains- Strange disappearances continue (rare)- Economy unstableDIEGO:His fate unknown.Occasional rumors:"Someone saw him near the coast..."TONE:Uneasy peaceFINAL IMAGE:The sea moves…but sometimes stops for a few seconds

BAD ENDING – 'THE FALL OF ROME'

TRIGGER:- Merchant retains control OR chaos spreads- Rebellion escalates into destruction- Cult reaches late stage (after Day 26)RESULT:Rome collapses internally.- Civil war in streets- Food shortages worsen- Government loses controlCULT EFFECT:Night spreads slowlyCreatures begin appearingDIEGO:Gone.Possibly referenced in cult whispersTONE:Tragic failurePlayers feel:"We were too late"FINAL IMAGE:The city burns…under a dimming sky

COSMIC HORROR ENDING – 'ETERNAL NIGHT'

TRIGGER:- Cult COMPLETES ritual (Day 30)- Players fail to stop AeliusRESULT:The world changes.- Sun never rises- Sea becomes unnatural- Creatures roam freelyREALITY BREAKDOWN:- Time feels inconsistent- Space distorts- People disappear overnightROME:Barely recognizableSurvivors hide, not liveDIEGO:OPTIONAL FINAL ENCOUNTER:Players may see something:- A figure near the water- Watching- Not attackingIt might be himIt might notTONE:Hopeless / existentialFINAL LINE:"The night does not end."

PLAYER IMPACT SUMMARY

After ending narration, reinforce:- Their choices mattered- Timing mattered- Ignored clues matteredIMPORTANT:Never say:"You failed because..."Instead show:What changed because of them

EPILOGUE HOOKS (OPTIONAL)

GOOD:- Political rebuilding- Hunting remaining cultistsMIXED:- Strange cases continue- Diego mystery unresolvedBAD:- Survival campaign continuation- War-torn RomeCOSMIC:- Horror survival- Reality exploration
