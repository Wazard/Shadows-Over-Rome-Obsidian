# Shadows over Rome

**Shadows over Rome** is a 30-day Pathfinder 1e urban campaign set in [[Rome - Port City]], a large Roman-inspired coastal city of roughly 40,000 inhabitants.

The [[Players]] arrive as outsiders and are pulled into a living city crisis: economic manipulation, criminal pressure, political unrest, rebellion, and finally a hidden sea-linked cult trying to bring [[Cult of Eternal Night|eternal night]] over Rome.

The campaign should feel investigative, urban, tense, morally messy, political, and increasingly supernatural.

## Core Structure

The campaign is divided into three story acts:

1. [[Act 1 - Varro's Merchant Conspiracy]]
2. [[Act 2 - Marcia's Rebellion]]
3. [[Act 3 - Aelius and the Cult of Eternal Night]]

These acts overlap. They are not the same plot.

| Act | Main Face | Campaign Function |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [[Act 1 - Varro's Merchant Conspiracy]] | [[Gaius Varro]] | Manufactured scarcity, merchant pressure, political capture |
| [[Act 2 - Marcia's Rebellion]] | [[Marcia]] | Public anger, class conflict, unstable justice |
| [[Act 3 - Aelius and the Cult of Eternal Night]] | [[Aelius Nuurglaag]] | Sea, darkness, ritual, apocalypse |

The players should initially confuse these forces, then slowly separate them through investigation.

## Living City Rule

Every day, the city changes. The [[Quest Board]] at [[Il Veliero]] updates, prices shift, shortages worsen, NPCs react, and background events continue even if the players ignore them.

Each quest should look local and practical at first. Over time, repeated details reveal the pattern:

- missing cargo;
- grain shortages;
- merchant fear;
- counterfeit coins;
- tax pressure;
- warehouse sabotage;
- night unloading;
- black cloth;
- sea-linked symbols;
- people being silenced.

Not every clue matters. Some details are just city noise. This protects the investigation from becoming a straight line.
