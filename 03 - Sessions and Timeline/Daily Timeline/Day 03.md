# Day 03

## Calendar And Festival Frame

- Date: Desnus 3, 3608.
- Festival state: Breaching Festival week. Markets, music, performers, pilgrims, and caravans crowd the city; every shortage is more public because everyone is in the streets.


Main beat: disappearances begin behind the economic noise.

- Primary quests: [[Merchant Refuses Delivery]], [[Official Transport Escort]]
- City mood: docks quieter, slums uneasy, market distracted.
- Varro pressure: protected routes and official-looking escorts become visible.
- Cult pressure: missing dock workers and water-adjacent traces appear, but should remain ambiguous.
