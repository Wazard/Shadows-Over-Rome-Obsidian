# Diego

Diego is a loud, emotional, conspiracy-minded NPC who works as a hidden signal inside city noise.

Most people treat him as unreliable. That is useful: he often notices real patterns, but explains them with the wrong theory, too much anger, and no clean structure.

## Table Function

- introduces true clues inside chaotic speech;
- creates doubt without confirming the cult plot;
- rewards attentive players who separate detail from interpretation;
- becomes more important after his disappearance.

## Behavior

- speaks loudly and interrupts;
- complains constantly;
- jumps from one topic to another;
- gets offended when ignored;
- never explains cleanly.

## Diego Clue Formula

Every Diego scene should contain:

- one true detail;
- one wrong interpretation;
- one emotional rant.

The players must separate signal from noise.

## Use Rules

- Do not overuse him.
- Do not confirm that he is right.
- Do not make him directly useful.
- Let the players ignore him early if they choose.

## Disappearance

Diego disappears during Act 2, around days 15-18. The disappearance should not be dramatic at first. No clear witness, no clean answer, only his notes.

The intended player feeling is: "We should have listened more carefully."
