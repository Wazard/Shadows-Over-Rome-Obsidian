# The City 
Main city note: [[Rome - Port City]]  
  
The city is a Roman-style coastal metropolis divided into several important districts.  
  
## Major Districts  
  
| District               | Function                                               | Main Notes                                       |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------ |
| [[Il Veliero]]         | Player hub and quest board                             | Tavern near the docks                            |
| [[Harbor District]]    | Ships, cargo, dockworkers, sea access                  | Missing cargo, sabotage, strange arrivals        |
| [[Warehouse District]] | Economic heart of the city                             | Grain, ledgers, guild politics, sabotage         |
| [[Merchant District]]  | House of the Merchant's Guild and various laboratirues | [[Merchnt Guild]] and various NPCs laboratories  |
| [[Market Square]]      | Public economy and city mood                           | Inflation, food scarcity, performers, riots      |
| [[Noble District]]     | Wealth, villas, hidden elven nobles                    | Luxury, intrigue, transmutation rings            |
| [[Government Hill]]    | State power and religion                               | [[Government Palace]] and [[Temple of Sarenrae]] |
| [[Military District]]  | Soldiers, barracks, training grounds                   | Order, repression, siege response                |
| [[Slums]]              | Poverty, crime, rebel sympathy                         | Half-orcs, desperation, street politics          |
| [[Black Market]]       | Criminal underworld                                    | Ruled by [[Arach-Sul]]                           |
