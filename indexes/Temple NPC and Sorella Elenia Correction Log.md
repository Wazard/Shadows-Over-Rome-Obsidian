# Temple NPC And Sorella Elenia Correction Log

## Canon Correction

`Aelia Polvere` and `Sorella Elenia` are separate NPCs.

| NPC | Correct identity |
| --- | --- |
| [[Aelia Polvere]] | Varro-linked hidden guard with a rare musket |
| [[Sorella Elenia]] | Temple of Sarenrae support NPC for care, food lines, wounds, donations, and moral pressure scenes |

## Renaming Decision

The temple sister previously appearing in Sarenrae encounter material under the confusing source name was renamed to [[Sorella Elenia]] to avoid conflict with [[Aelia Polvere]].

## Link Fixes Made

- Replaced active play-facing Sarenrae encounter links from `[[Aelia Polvere|Sorella Aelia]]` to `[[Sorella Elenia]]`.
- Renamed the active Sarenrae random encounter files and headings from the old temple-sister name to `Sorella Elenia`.
- Updated frontmatter `npc` fields in those encounters to `Sorella Elenia`.
- Updated encounter prose shorthand from `Aelia` to `Elenia` where it referred to the temple sister.
- Updated Session 02 prep to borrow from the corrected Day 01 evening encounter.
- Corrected the earlier Day 1-2 cleanup log row that had wrongly mapped the temple sister to [[Aelia Polvere]].
