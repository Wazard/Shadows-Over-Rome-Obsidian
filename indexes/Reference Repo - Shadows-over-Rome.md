## Rule

Use `Wazard/Shadows-over-Rome` only as source material. Apply changes only in this Obsidian vault.

## Reference Repo Shape

- `phase 1`: Act 1 DM guide, quests, plans, props, routines, and generators.
- `phase 2`: Act 2 material for rebellion and later city escalation.
- `phase 3`: Act 3 material for Aelius, siege, final confrontation, and endgame.
- `Apps/CSVs`: encounter, routine, NPC, market, and quest board data.
- `images`: sigils, maps, Il Veliero images, and other visual props.
- root `.docx` files: campaign endings, NPC lists, player handout, sea/failure feedback system, sigils.
