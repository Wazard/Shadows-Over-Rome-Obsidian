Phase 2 – Day 13 Secondary Quest: The Drowned Shrine

This quest foreshadows the cult’s ritual site.It should feel dangerous, incomplete, and not meant to be fully explored yet.Players can investigate, but not resolve it.

HOOK – QUEST BOARD / RUMOR

"Something appears at low tide."Fishermen report:- A structure visible only when the water recedes- Strange lights at night- No one willing to approach closelyOptional NPC:"I've sailed these waters for years… that wasn't there before."

LOCATION – COASTAL CLIFFS (LOW TIDE)

The shrine becomes visible only briefly.Appearance:- Stone structure partially submerged- Covered in algae and salt- Unnaturally intactIMPORTANT:Water level feels wrong, not natural tide behavior

ARRIVAL

Atmosphere:- Wind inconsistent- Sound dampened- Sea unnaturally stillDETAILS:- No wildlife nearby- Footprints rarely present- Air feels heavyDM NOTE:Players should feel uneasy immediately

SHRINE STRUCTURE

Players can explore:1) OUTER PLATFORM- Slippery stone- Carved symbols (large scale)2) ENTRY HALL- Narrow, partially flooded- Walls covered in markings3) LOWER CHAMBER (LIMITED ACCESS)- Mostly submerged- Too dangerous to fully exploreIMPORTANT:Do not allow full exploration safely

CLUES

- Symbols identical to earlier ones (larger, deliberate)- Black wax residue- Carvings depicting figures facing the seaNEW DETAIL:Symbols form patterns (not random)IMPLICATION:This is a designed ritual space

CORE EVENTS

Choose 1–2:1) WATER MOVEMENT- Water shifts without wind2) SHADOW BELOW- Something large moves under surface3) SOUND- Low, rhythmic “breathing”4) LIGHT FLICKER- Faint glow from deeper chamberDM NOTE:Never reveal the source

ENCOUNTERS

OPTIONAL:- 1–2 Drowned Entities- Slow, unnatural movement- Retreat into water if pressuredENVIRONMENTAL DANGER:- Rising water- Slippery footing- Sudden waves (unnatural)GOAL:Pressure players to leave

LIMITATION – WHY THEY CAN’T FINISH IT

The shrine becomes inaccessible due to:- Rising water- Structural collapse risk- Increasing dangerIMPORTANT:Players must feel:"We could come back later"DM NOTE:This is intentional foreshadowing

WHAT PLAYERS LEARN

- The sea is part of something bigger- Symbols are not random- There is a deeper structure belowMISLEADING CONCLUSIONS:- Ancient ruin- Lost temple- Natural phenomenonTRUE MEANING:This is tied to the final ritual site

AFTERMATH

When players return later:- Shrine is gone (submerged)OR- Less visible than beforeFINAL DETAIL:Water returns to unnatural stillnessDM LINE:"It’s like it was never meant to be seen for long."

DM NOTES

Purpose:- Foreshadow final dungeon (Sea Caves)- Reinforce sea horror- Build anticipationDO NOT:- Allow full exploration- Reveal cult directly- Resolve mysteryKEY FEELING:"We are not ready for this place."
