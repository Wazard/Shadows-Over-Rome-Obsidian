Day 16 – Diego Kidnapping Scene (Playable Sequence)

This is a real-time playable event.Players can interfere, fight, and track Diego.They should feel like they ALMOST saved him.He is NOT salvageable.

TRIGGER – STARTING THE SCENE

Location: TAVERN / CITY

Trigger this when:- Players ask about Diego- OR visit docks/cliffs- OR enough time has passed in the dayNPC INFO:- "He hasn’t been seen since yesterday"- "He was heading to the coast again"OPTIONAL HOOK:A fisherman says:"He was arguing with someone… but I didn’t see anyone."DM NOTE:Create urgency but no certainty.

MORNING – TRACKING DIEGO

Location: DOCKS

Players can investigate:CLUES:- Diego’s footprints (erratic, pacing)- Symbols scratched repeatedly- One dropped note: "Too late?"ENCOUNTER:2 Dock ThugsDIALOGUE:- "You shouldn’t be asking questions"COMBAT:Easy fightIMPORTANT:One thug tries to ESCAPE toward cliffsDM NOTE:If players stop him → gain time advantage laterIf he escapes → cult is alerted early

AFTERNOON – MOVEMENT TO CLIFFS

Location: TRADE ROADS → COASTAL CLIFFS

Atmosphere:- Wind stronger than usual- Fewer civilians- Tension risingENCOUNTER:Cult Scout (1)BEHAVIOR:- Observes from distance- Runs if spottedIF CAUGHT:- "You are already seen."DM NOTE:This determines how prepared the cult is later.

LATE AFTERNOON – CLIFFS

Location: COASTAL CLIFFS

Players find:- Clear struggle signs- Blood drops (not lethal)- Drag marks downwardIMPORTANT DETAIL:Diego resisted HARDAUDIO:Faint chanting belowDM NOTE:Give players a moment to realize they are close.

DESCENT – THE RACE

Location: CLIFF PATH → SEA CAVES

This is a TIME-SENSITIVE sequence.Players descend while:- Hearing movement ahead- Seeing flickering lightOBSTACLES:- Narrow path (DEX checks optional)- Falling rocks- Slippery groundFAILURES:- Slow players downSUCCESS:- They arrive earlier (but still too late)DM NOTE:Make them feel like speed matters.

FIRST CONTACT

Location: SEA CAVE ENTRANCE

Players see:- 3 cultists dragging Diego- Diego is conscious but weakDIEGO:- "NO—NO—DON’T COME DOWN!"COMBAT STARTSENEMIES:- 3 Cultists (light)- 1 Enforcer (stronger)GOAL:Cultists delay, NOT winIMPORTANT:After 2 rounds → reinforcements approachingDM NOTE:Players should win this fight.

MID SCENE – FALSE HOPE

Location: CAVE MIDWAY

If players win quickly:They can reach Diego.INTERACTION:Diego grabs a player:- "Non dovevate venire… è troppo tardi…"He is TERRIFIED.THEN:Chanting intensifies deeper inside.NEW ENEMIES ARRIVE:- 4 cultists- 1 marked cultist (symbol carved into skin)DM NOTE:This creates the illusion they can still save him.

FINAL MOMENT – THE LOSS

Location: DEEP CAVE EDGE

As players push forward:They reach a ritual chamber edge.THEY SEE:- Diego being pulled into circle- Aelius present (at distance, not fully engaged)IMPORTANT:Players can:- Dash- Attack- InterruptBUT:RITUAL TRIGGERS ANYWAYEVENT:- Light distortion- Sound collapse- Diego disappears / is taken / transformed out of reachNO BODY LEFTDM DESCRIPTION:"You're just a moment too late."Make it feel like:- One more turn could have changed it

FINAL ENCOUNTER

Location: CAVE EXIT

After ritual:Enemies panic / withdrawORFight defensivelyENEMIES:- Remaining cultists- Possibly unstable magic effectsOPTION:Players can pursue → leads nowhereDM NOTE:Do NOT allow reaching Diego

AFTERMATH

Location: CLIFFS / RETURN

Silence.No Diego.Only:- Marks on ground- Residual symbol- Echo of chantingPLAYER FEELING:"I almost had him"IMPORTANT:If they performed well:Reward with:- Extra clues- More knowledgeBUT NEVER:Success in saving Diego

DM DESIGN NOTES

Location: DM ONLY

WHY THIS WORKS:- Players win fights → feel capable- They track correctly → feel smart- They move fast → feel efficientBUT:Outcome is locked by timing illusionKEY TECHNIQUE:Always let them be:"One step behind, not blocked"Never:"You can't do this"Always:"You almost did"
