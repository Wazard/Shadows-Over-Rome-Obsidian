Phase 1 – Day 7 Secondary Quest: The Tax Collector

This quest introduces Varro’s indirect control over the city.It presents a moral choice: support order or expose corruption.Nothing supernatural is involved.

HOOK – QUEST BOARD

"Assistance Required – Tax Enforcement"A government tax agent requires assistance collecting overdue paymentsin the Market District and surrounding areas.Some citizens have resisted.Payment guaranteed upon completion.

KEY NPC – TAX COLLECTOR

Name: Cassian RulfoRole:Official tax agentPersonality:- Calm- Polite but firm- Never raises voiceDialogue:- "I am only doing my duty."- "The system must function."- "Without order, there is nothing."IMPORTANT:He believes he is justified

HIDDEN TRUTH

Cassian is secretly working under Varro’s influence.Taxes are:- Increased unfairly- Targeting specific individuals- Used to consolidate controlHe may not fully realize the extent of corruption

QUEST FLOW

TAVERN → MARKET DISTRICT → MULTIPLE COLLECTION POINTSPlayers accompany Cassian while he collects payments.They witness:- Fear- Resentment- Quiet resistance

COLLECTION POINT 1 – SMALL SHOP

Owner:Elderly shopkeeperSituation:Cannot pay full taxDialogue:- "I already paid last month…"- "This isn’t right"CASSIAN:- Calmly insists on paymentPLAYER OPTIONS:- Force payment- Cover cost- Let it goCLUE:Receipt shows altered numbers

COLLECTION POINT 2 – FAMILY HOME

Situation:Family hiding valuablesTENSION:- Emotional scene- Children presentCASSIAN:- Still calm- Applies pressureOPTIONAL EVENT:Neighbor intervenesCLUE:Tax targets seem selective, not random

COLLECTION POINT 3 – MERCHANT

Merchant reacts aggressivelyDIALOGUE:- "This isn’t tax—it’s theft!"ESCALATION:- Possible fight (non-lethal)IMPORTANT:Merchant mentions Varro indirectly:"Funny how all this started when HE got involved"CLUE:Direct link to Varro influence

INVESTIGATION OPTIONS

Players may:- Examine records- Question Cassian- Compare tax demandsDISCOVER:- Inconsistent taxation- Targeted individuals- Increased rates without explanationIMPORTANT:Corruption is systemic, not chaotic

PLAYER CHOICES

1) SUPPORT CASSIAN- Help enforce collections- Maintain orderRESULT:- Citizens suffer- Varro influence grows2) OPPOSE CASSIAN- Refuse to collect- Protect citizensRESULT:- Conflict with authorities- Reduced trust in system3) EXPOSE CORRUPTION- Present evidence- Turn people against systemRESULT:- Cassian shaken- Seeds rebellion support

ENCOUNTERS

- Angry civilians (social conflict)- Merchant confrontation (possible combat)- Guards arriving if situation escalatesCOMBAT STYLE:- Short- Chaotic- Avoid lethal outcomes if possible

OUTCOMES

If players SUPPORT:- Varro gains strength- Citizens grow resentfulIf players OPPOSE:- Public support increases- Authorities take noticeIf players EXPOSE:- Cassian questions his role- Rumors spread quicklyIMPACT:Affects merchant power variable in ending

ATMOSPHERE

Tone:- Grounded- Tense- UncomfortableNo horror here—only human pressurePlayers should feel:"This is wrong… but legal"

DM NOTES

Purpose:- Show Varro’s control without showing Varro- Create moral ambiguity- Ground the campaign before escalationIMPORTANT:Do NOT make Cassian evilHe is part of the systemFINAL LINE OPTION:Cassian, quietly:"If this is wrong… then everything is."
