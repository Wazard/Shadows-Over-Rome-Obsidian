# Workers Missing from the Docks

Day 3 Quest – Workers Missing from the Docks

This quest investigates the disappearance of dock workers.It should feel grounded, uncertain, and slightly unsettling.The supernatural element is optional and only occurs if players linger too long.

HOOK – QUEST BOARD

"Workers missing from the docks."Several dock workers have failed to report for duty.Last seen near late-night unloading operations.Pay: 11 silverDuration: ~5 hours

START – TAVERN / DOCKS

Workers and sailors are already talking about it.NPC (Dock Foreman):"They don’t just vanish. Not like this."Tone:Concerned, not panicked

LOCATION FLOW

DOCKS → STORAGE AREAS → BACK ALLEYS → WATER EDGEOptional extension:COASTAL PATH (if players track further)

DOCKS INVESTIGATION

Players can:- Question workers- Check last known shifts- Inspect unloading zonesCLUES:- Workers last seen handling specific cargo- Same merchant seal as previous quest- One worker mentioned "strange crates"DIALOGUE:"They were working late. Didn’t finish unloading."

STORAGE AREA

Signs of disturbance:- Crates slightly moved- One broken openINSIDE:- Salt residue- Black wax traces (symbol partially visible)CLUES:- Same material as missing cargo quest- Workers likely interacted with this cargo

TRACKING THE MISSING WORKERS

Players find:- Drag marks- Footprints (multiple directions)Important:Some tracks lead toward:- Water edgeOthers:- Into narrow alleysINTERPRETATION OPTIONS:- Abduction- Escape- Conflict

WATER EDGE

Evidence:- Scuffed ground- Rope fibers- Blood traces (small amounts)SEA DETAIL:Water unnaturally stillIf observed closely:Ripples behave slightly delayedCLUE:Something was taken… or something entered the water

OPTIONAL ENCOUNTER – REBEL CONTACT

Players may encounter a rebel sympathizer.DIALOGUE:"They weren’t taken by us.""They were messing with things they shouldn’t have touched."Ambiguous:Rebels deny involvement

OPTIONAL CULT HINT

If players inspect symbols:They notice:- Same recurring mark- Carved, not paintedCLUE:This is intentional marking, not random graffiti

TIME TRIGGER – IF PLAYERS STAY +3 HOURS

Late night.The docks grow silent.EVENT:The missing workers return.But:- Slow movement- Unresponsive- Eyes unfocusedThey begin walking…Direction:Toward the coastal path → abandoned chapelIMPORTANT:They do NOT attack unless provoked

UNDEAD ENCOUNTER (OPTIONAL)

If players intervene:Enemies:- 2–4 reanimated workers (reskinned zombies, CR 1/4)Behavior:- Ignore players initially- Continue moving toward chapel- Attack only if blocked or harmedGOAL:Create unease, not combat focus

PLAYER CHOICES (UNDEAD EVENT)

Players may:- Follow them- Stop them- Ignore themIMPORTANT:Stopping them reveals nothing immediateFollowing them leads toward:Future location (chapel)

CLUE SUMMARY

Players may uncover:- Workers tied to specific cargo- Same merchant seal recurring- Strange residue (salt + wax)- Tracks toward water and inland- Optional: unnatural behavior (if event triggers)INTERPRETATIONS:- Rebel involvement- Smuggling gone wrong- Something worse (unclear)

DIEGO INTERACTION

If told about missing workers:DIEGO:"Yeah, they disappear. Then what? Nobody asks?"If told about them returning:(long pause)"...No. That’s not normal. That’s really not normal."

OUTCOMES & FFS IMPACT

IGNORE / FAIL:+1 Cult ProgressINVESTIGATE THOROUGHLY:No immediate shift, but unlocks pattern recognitionFOLLOW UNDEAD:Early access to chapel clue chain

NEXT DAY EFFECTS

- More missing person rumors- Dock workers uneasy- Fewer people working night shiftsIf undead event triggered:- Rumors of "people walking at night"- Dismissed by most NPCs

DM NOTES

This is a transition quest.DO:- Keep main focus grounded- Make undead event optional and eerieDO NOT:- Turn this into a combat-heavy quest- Confirm supernatural explanationGOAL FEELING:"Something is very wrong… but we don’t understand it yet."
