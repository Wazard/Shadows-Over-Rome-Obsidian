# Quest Board

The quest board at [[Il Veliero]] is the campaign's daily pressure valve. It should feel practical, local, and a little messy: missing cargo, escort work, mediation, rumors, low-pay errands, and urgent notices.

The players should not see "main quests." They should see jobs.

## Act 1 Function

During [[Act 1 - Varro's Merchant Conspiracy]], the board slowly teaches the players that Rome's problems repeat:

- cargo is missing or delayed;
- merchants disagree about records;
- prices change without clear reason;
- workers disappear;
- official jobs pay too well;
- desperate people ask for help without enough money;
- Diego's warnings look ridiculous until they do not.
