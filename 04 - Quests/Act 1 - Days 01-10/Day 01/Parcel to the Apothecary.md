# Parcel to the Apothecary

Board hook: parcel needed from the harbor - delivery to apothecary.

This is a simple movement quest for Day 1. It moves the players between the port, market, and inner streets while showing small delays and logistical irritation.

## Quest Board Text

Pick up a small sealed parcel at the commercial pier and deliver it to the apothecary near the market. Payment on delivery. Do not open.

Posted by: [[Publius Nerva]] or his assistant.  
Payment: low.  
Expected duration: 45-90 minutes.

## Step 1 - Pickup At The Port

The parcel is small, wrapped in waxed cloth, and tied with simple cord. It is not illegal, not meaningfully magical, and not directly connected to Varro.

[[Port Assistant]] is young, tired, and rushed.

Sample lines:

- "For the apothecary, yes? Sign here and bring it whole."
- "No, I do not know what is inside. No, I do not want to know."
- "Today everyone asks where things are. I only know where they should be."

## Step 2 - Travel Event

Use one, not all.

| Event | Function |
| --- | --- |
| Blocked street | Two cart drivers argue over who has right of way; shows logistical disorder |
| Curious child | Asks if the parcel contains medicine, bread, or poison; lightens the tone |
| Light guard check | A bored guard asks what they carry; shows the city checking more without drama |

## Step 3 - Delivery

The apothecary smells of herbs, vinegar, and oils. [[Market Apothecary]] is precise, irritated, and middle class.

Sample lines:

- "Finally. This was supposed to arrive this morning."
- "No, not with you. With the port. Always with the port."
- "If even small things arrive late, imagine the important ones."

If the players open the parcel:

- Contents: vials, dried herbs, and a small ingredient list.
- Medicine or Nature DC 12: ingredients for common remedies.
- Investigation DC 14: one vial is missing from the list. This is probably a preparation or supply error, not a major mystery.

## Outcomes

- Intact delivery: full payment.
- Opened but complete delivery: reduced payment, irritated apothecary.
- Lost or broken delivery: no payment, no major consequence.

## GM Notes

Do not turn this into contraband. Do not add cultists or symbols. Its job is rhythm, movement, and atmosphere.

Feeling: "Even ordinary things are working worse than normal."
