Source file: `phase 2/Shadows_Over_Rome_Phase2_DM_Guide.docx`

Shadows Over Rome – Phase 2 DM Deep Guide (Days 11–20)

Phase 2 is escalation.The city is unstable, violence increases, and misinformation spreads.IMPORTANT:- Players will likely blame the wrong faction- Let that happen- The cult benefits from chaos

Day 11

Situation Overview

People begin fleeing the city. Fear replaces confusion.

NPC Behavior

- Varro: enforcing order through guards

- Marcia: organizing resistance cells

- Aelius: increasing abductions quietly

- Diego: increasingly agitated

Clues Available

- Civilians afraid of guards, not rebels

- Rumors of people disappearing at night

- Same symbol seen in multiple districts

Encounters

- Escort civilians encounter

- Guard harassment scene

- Refugee panic moment

Zone Activity

- Slums: partially controlled by rebels

- Market: guarded and tense

- Roads: filling with refugees

Main Events

- Population movement begins

- Authority becomes aggressive

Secondary Events

- Families splitting up

- First abandoned homes
