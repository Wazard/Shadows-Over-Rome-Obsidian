# Shadows Over Rome Phase3 DM Guide

Source file: `phase 3/Shadows_Over_Rome_Phase3_DM_Guide.docx`

Shadows Over Rome – Phase 3 DM Deep Guide (Days 21–30)

Phase 3 is collapse.The world stops behaving normally.Truth is no longer hidden—it is overwhelming.IMPORTANT:- Do not over-explain- Let players feel loss of control- Use silence and description more than action

Day 21

Situation Overview

The world begins to feel wrong. Nature stops behaving normally.

NPC Behavior

- Varro: overwhelmed, losing control

- Marcia: confused, still blaming merchant

- Aelius: fully focused on ritual

- Diego: missing, but notes circulate

Clues Available

- Water unnaturally still

- Dead animals with black veins

- Symbol carved into coastal rock

Encounters

- Strange creature encounter (not fully visible)

- Environmental tension (silence, no wind)

Zone Activity

- Docks: abandoned

- Coast: unnatural calm

- City: uneasy quiet

Main Events

- Ritual energy leaking into world

- Nature destabilized

Secondary Events

- Fishermen refuse to work

- Animals behave erratically
