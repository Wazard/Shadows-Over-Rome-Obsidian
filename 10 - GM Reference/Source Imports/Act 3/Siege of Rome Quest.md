# Siege of Rome Quest

Source file: `phase 3/Siege_of_Rome_Quest.docx`

Phase 2 – Siege of Rome: Major Branching Quest

This quest becomes available during Phase 2 when the rebellion begins the siege of Rome.Players can choose to SUPPORT the rebellion or HELP LIFT the siege.A sub-quest allows re-entry into the city if players are outside.

QUEST OVERVIEW

This is a branching quest with consequences.CHOICES:1) Support the Rebellion2) Help Lift the Siege3) Re-enter the City (sub-quest, no main impact)The siege progresses regardless, but player actions influence:- Duration- Casualties- Political outcome

PATH 1 – SUPPORT THE REBELLION

HOOK:Posted anonymously or via rebel contact."Supplies and hands needed. The city must fall."GOAL:Help rebels weaken Rome’s defenses.OBJECTIVES:- Smuggle weapons to siege camps- Sabotage city gates or supply lines- Assist in coordinated assaultENCOUNTERS:- Rebel fighters (organized, disciplined)- City patrol ambushes- Night infiltration missionsKEY NPC:Rebel Leader (Marcia)DIALOGUE:- "We are not destroyers. We are correction."- "Rome forgot its people."OUTCOME:If successful:- Siege intensifies- Government destabilizes fasterIf failed:- Rebellion suffers losses- Siege continues but weakerIMPACT:Affects final ending (rebellion strength)

PATH 2 – HELP LIFT THE SIEGE

HOOK:City officials or merchants."Rome needs defenders. Supplies are running thin."GOAL:Stabilize the city and repel the rebellion.OBJECTIVES:- Escort supply caravans into city- Repair defenses- Defend key gates during assaultsENCOUNTERS:- Rebel attack squads- Saboteurs inside city- Civil unrestKEY NPC:City Commander / Varro-aligned officerDIALOGUE:- "Order must hold."- "If Rome falls, everything falls."OUTCOME:If successful:- Siege weakens- City holds longerIf failed:- Food shortages worsen- Panic spreadsIMPACT:Affects final ending (city stability)

SUB-QUEST – RE-ENTER THE CITY

TRIGGER:Players are outside the walls during siege.GOAL:Find a way back inside Rome.IMPORTANT:This quest has NO impact on main story outcome.METHODS:1) SECRET PASSAGE- Old tunnel under walls- Guarded by 2–3 enemies or blocked2) BRIBE / DISGUISE- Convince guards or sneak in- Social or stealth challenge3) SCALE WALLS- Night climb- Risk of falling or detectionENCOUNTERS:- Patrols- Suspicious guards- Environmental hazardsOUTCOME:Players re-enter city successfully OR fail and must retry.NO IMPACT on:- Siege progress- Ending conditionsDM NOTE:Purely logistical quest

ATMOSPHERE – THE SIEGE

Inside City:- Food shortages- Fear and tension- Guards everywhereOutside City:- Rebel camps- Fires at night- Organized military presenceGeneral Tone:- Pressure- Urgency- Moral ambiguity

CROSSOVER WITH OTHER EVENTS

Rebels may unknowingly use:- Undead-supplied goods (necromancer quest)Merchant influence:- May manipulate both sidesCult:- Not directly involved here (yet)

DM NOTES

This quest defines player alignment in Phase 2.Do NOT present one side as purely good.Rebels:- Justified but dangerousCity:- Stable but corruptSub-quest exists to:- Avoid locking players out- Maintain pacingIMPORTANT:Main quest paths MUST influence ending.Sub-quest MUST NOT.
