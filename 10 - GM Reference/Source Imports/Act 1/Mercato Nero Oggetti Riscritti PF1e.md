Mantello di seta ragnesca

Tipo

Wondrous item minore

Luogo

Mercato nero interno

Venditore

Arach-Sul il Tessitore

Prezzo aggiornato

900 silver

Uso consigliato

Furtività in tunnel, sotterranei, passaggi stretti.

Descrizione superficiale

Mantello quasi privo di peso, in seta nera con riflessi blu come ragnatela bagnata. L’orlo non raccoglie fango, ma trattiene polvere, capelli sottili e fili scuri che non sembrano appartenere al tessuto.

Proprieta da identificato

Aura faint illusion and transmutation; CL 3rd; Slot shoulders; Price 900 gp; Weight 1 lb.

Description

This cloak grants a +2 competence bonus on Stealth checks while the wearer is in tunnels, sewers, caves, crawlspaces, alleys with walls close enough to touch, or other tight enclosed passages. The bonus does not apply in open streets, bright noble halls, wide courtyards, or open wilderness.

Drawback

Vermin and small crawling creatures show abnormal interest in the wearer. When the wearer fails a Stealth check by 5 or more in an appropriate environment, the GM may add a minor tell: twitching silk, black threads catching on stone, or insects gathering near the hem.

Identification

Spellcraft DC 18 identifies the stealth property. Knowledge (dungeoneering) or Sense Motive DC 20 suggests that the cloak is not neutral to tunnel creatures.

Anello del debito silenzioso

Tipo

Cursed ring

Luogo

Mercato nero interno

Venditore

Arach-Sul il Tessitore

Prezzo aggiornato

850 silver

Uso consigliato

Protezione limitata da interrogatori e magie di verità.

Descrizione superficiale

Anello sottile di ferro annerito. All’interno corre un filo pallido, simile a seta di ragno, teso in un solco tanto fine da sembrare una clausola incisa più che un ornamento.

Proprieta da identificato

Aura faint abjuration and divination; CL 3rd; Slot ring; Price 850 gp; Weight -.

Description

Once per week, when the wearer attempts a saving throw against a truth effect, minor compulsion, magical interrogation, or similar effect of 2nd level or lower, the wearer may gain a +4 resistance bonus on that saving throw. This decision must be made before the result is known.

Curse

When the ring is used, Arach-Sul senses its activation and gains a vague impression of the wearer’s current category of location: market, surface city, outside the walls, temple district, noble district, or underground. This does not give distance or precise direction.

Identification

Spellcraft DC 19 identifies the protective abjuration. Spellcraft or Sense Motive DC 24 reveals the tracking clause hidden in the enchantment.

Filo di ritorno del Tessitore

Tipo

Wondrous item

Luogo

Mercato nero interno

Venditore

Arach-Sul il Tessitore

Prezzo aggiornato

600 silver

Uso consigliato

Navigazione nei tunnel del mercato nero.

Descrizione superficiale

Rocchetto minuscolo di filo nero, più sottile della seta e più resistente della corda. Quando viene srotolato, il filo non tocca mai davvero il pavimento: resta appena sollevato, come se ricordasse la via.

Proprieta da identificato

Aura faint divination; CL 3rd; Slot none; Price 600 gp; Weight -.

Description

Once per day, while within the known tunnels of the black market or an attached underground passage, the bearer may command the thread to indicate the safest known route to the nearest exit already connected to the market’s traffic. It does not reveal treasure, secret rooms, forbidden doors, or unknown exits. Following it during calm conditions requires no check; during pursuit or panic, a Survival or Perception check DC 18 prevents delays.

Drawback

If the bearer knowingly breaks a bargain made under the market’s protection, the thread turns gray and snaps. Guides and brokers of the market recognize the broken thread as a mark of unreliability.

Identification

Spellcraft or Knowledge (local) DC 18 identifies its navigational function. Sense Motive DC 19 reveals that it is also a commercial token of trust.

Prezzo aggiornato

Uso consigliato

Trattative, pesi, provenienza e piccole bugie commerciali.

Descrizione superficiale

Bilancina in ottone scuro con due piatti grandi quanto monete. L’ago centrale trema anche quando il tavolo è fermo, come se pesasse parole invece di merci.

Proprieta da identificato

Aura faint divination; CL 3rd; Slot none; Price 750 gp; Weight 1 lb.

Description

Once per commercial scene, when a creature within 30 feet states a simple factual lie about price, weight, quantity, ownership, or provenance, the scale trembles. It does not reveal omissions, emotional lies, disguised intent, or complex conspiracies.

Drawback

Using the scale openly is socially aggressive. A proud or dangerous merchant usually treats it as an accusation; the GM may impose a -2 penalty on Diplomacy checks with that merchant during the scene unless the user succeeds at Diplomacy DC 16.

Identification

Spellcraft or Appraise DC 18 identifies its limited divination. Sense Motive DC 17 reveals that it detects only small practical lies.

Cursed wondrous item

Venditore

Sibilla Rossa

Prezzo aggiornato

1,200 silver

Uso consigliato

Fingere la morte, evitare cattura, ingannare esami rapidi.

Descrizione superficiale

Amuleto di vetro scuro e rame con una goccia rossa sospesa al centro. La goccia pulsa solo quando nessuno la guarda direttamente.

Proprieta da identificato

Aura faint necromancy and illusion; CL 5th; Slot neck; Price 1,200 gp; Weight -.

Description

Once per day, the wearer may enter a deathlike trance for 1d4+1 minutes. During this time the wearer appears dead to mundane inspection. A Heal or Perception check DC 22 reveals the deception. Effects that clearly detect life, souls, or active magic may bypass the disguise. The wearer remains aware in a muffled way but cannot act until the effect ends or is dismissed.

Curse

The night after each use, the wearer dreams of being opened on a surgical table. The wearer must succeed at a Will save DC 13 or take a -1 morale penalty on Perception checks until midday.

Identification

Heal DC 16 or Spellcraft DC 19 identifies the death-feigning property. Heal DC 20 reveals the traumatic residue.

Proprieta da identificato

School enchantment (emotion, mind-affecting); Level bard 1, cleric/oracle 1, inquisitor 1, paladin 1; Price 40 gp; Weight -.

Activation drink; Duration 10 minutes or until discharged.

Description

The drinker gains a +2 alchemical bonus on the next saving throw against fear or the next opposed check against Intimidate made within 10 minutes. Once the bonus is applied, the effect ends. This is not remove fear and does not suppress an ongoing fear condition.

Side Effect

After the effect ends, the drinker becomes twitchy and too loud. For 1 hour, the drinker takes a -2 penalty on Stealth and Bluff checks made in calm or quiet scenes. Fortitude DC 14 negates mild nausea that would otherwise last 1 minute after the bonus is discharged.

Identification

Craft (alchemy) DC 16 identifies the tonic. Craft (alchemy) DC 21 identifies the unstable stimulant residue.

Luogo

Mercato nero interno

Venditore

Eshka Vetro-Nero

Prezzo aggiornato

75 silver

Uso consigliato

Arma temporaneamente magica contro riduzioni del danno.

Descrizione superficiale

Ampolla di vetro pregiato con olio argentato. Il vetro riflette la lama prima ancora di essere aperto, come se l’arma fosse già un poco più vera.

Proprieta da identificato

School transmutation; Level cleric 1, magus 1, paladin 1, sorcerer/wizard 1; CL 1st; Price 75 gp; Weight -.

Activation apply to weapon; Duration 1 minute.

Description

This oil functions as magic weapon, except it may be applied to a single weapon as a standard action. The weapon gains a +1 enhancement bonus on attack and damage rolls for 1 minute.

Note

The price includes 25 gp for the expensive black-market glass. Applying it during stress requires Sleight of Hand or Craft (weapons) DC 12; failure means application takes 1 additional round.

Identification

Spellcraft DC 16 identifies the effect. Appraise DC 12 identifies the inflated glass cost.

Tipo

Cursed wondrous item minore

Luogo

Mercato nero interno

Venditore

Olma la Tessicarne

Prezzo aggiornato

250 silver

Uso consigliato

Resistere alla paura legata a non morti e necromanzia.

Descrizione superficiale

Vertebra levigata e forata, appesa a un filo nero. È troppo leggera, come se fosse vuota anche spiritualmente.

Proprieta da identificato

Aura faint necromancy; CL 3rd; Slot neck; Price 250 gp; Weight -.

Description

Once per day, the wearer gains a +2 resistance bonus on the first saving throw against fear caused by undead, necromancy, grave sites, corpse magic, or similar deathly supernatural scenes. The bonus is automatic and then discharged for the day.

Curse

If the amulet is worn during sleep, the wearer dreams of burial pits. Will DC 12 prevents a -1 penalty on Diplomacy checks with grieving, mourning, or recently bereaved people until midday.

Identification

Knowledge (religion) DC 16 or Spellcraft DC 17 identifies the deathward property. Knowledge (religion) DC 21 identifies that it keeps death close rather than driving it away.

Mercato nero interno

Venditore

Olma la Tessicarne

Prezzo aggiornato

500 silver

Uso consigliato

Allarme locale e disturbante.

Descrizione superficiale

Occhio essiccato e trattato, incastonato in rame. La pupilla non punta mai esattamente nella stessa direzione per più di qualche respiro.

Proprieta da identificato

Aura faint abjuration and divination; CL 3rd; Slot none; Price 500 gp; Weight -.

Description

When placed on a surface and commanded, the eye watches a 20-foot-radius area for up to 8 hours. If a Small or larger creature enters the area, the eye turns toward the intruder and weeps dark fluid. It does not transmit information at a distance and makes no sound.

Defect

Each 8-hour use has a 20% chance of a false positive caused by rats, dripping water, moving shadows, or similar minor disturbances. Interpreting the signal correctly in a tense scene requires Perception DC 15.

Identification

Spellcraft or Perception DC 18 identifies the alarm function. Sense Motive or Spellcraft DC 21 reveals unreliability.

Venditore

Olma la Tessicarne

Prezzo aggiornato

750 silver

Uso consigliato

Restare cosciente per un ultimo round.

Descrizione superficiale

Talismano di pelle trattata, filo nero e piccola placca d’osso. Sembra obbligare la carne a restare insieme, più che guarirla.

Proprieta da identificato

Aura faint necromancy; CL 3rd; Slot neck; Price 750 gp; Weight -.

Description

This talisman functions once. When the wearer would fall unconscious because of hit point loss, he may remain conscious and able to act for 1 additional round. At the end of that round, normal consequences apply. After triggering, the talisman blackens and becomes nonmagical.

Guanto del paracolpo

Tipo

Cursed defensive wondrous item

Luogo

Mercato nero interno

Venditore

Zhara delle Lame Basse

Prezzo aggiornato

1,200 silver

Uso consigliato

Difesa da duellante al posto di uno scudo vero.

Descrizione superficiale

Guanto pesante di cuoio e acciaio, con placche sovrapposte sulle nocche. È pratico, ma molto vistoso: sembra più una dichiarazione che una protezione.

Proprieta da identificato

Aura faint abjuration; CL 3rd; Slot hands; Price 1,200 gp; Weight 2 lbs.

Description

When worn on a free hand, the gauntlet grants a +1 shield bonus to AC, as a buckler. It imposes the normal practical limits of using a protected off-hand at the GM’s discretion and does not stack with an actual shield.

Curse

The gauntlet belonged to a duelist obsessed with honorable defense. Whenever the wearer is hit while wearing it, he must succeed at Fortitude DC 10 + the damage dealt/5 or take 1d3 additional nonlethal damage from the gauntlet’s punishing recoil.

Identification

Spellcraft DC 17 or Knowledge (martial) DC 15 identifies the parrying magic. Spellcraft DC 22 identifies the recoil curse.

Tipo

Cursed single-use sheet music

Luogo

Mercato nero interno

Venditore

Lyr Mezza-Notte

Prezzo aggiornato

150 silver

Uso consigliato

Una bugia preparata resa più credibile.

Descrizione superficiale

Foglio sottile con note musicali in inchiostro blu-nero. Le note sembrano cambiare se lette sottovoce, ma tornano innocenti appena qualcuno chiede spiegazioni.

Proprieta da identificato

Aura faint enchantment; CL 1st; Slot none; Price 150 gp; Weight -.

Activation perform, recite, or hum before speaking; Duration until the prepared lie is told or 10 minutes.

Description

Once, if the user rehearses a specific lie and performs the lullaby immediately before telling it, he gains a +2 competence bonus on one Bluff check made to deliver that prepared lie. The effect does not help improvisation, magical commands, or lies the user has not consciously prepared. After use, the sheet fades.

Identification

Perform DC 16 or Spellcraft DC 18 identifies the enchantment. Sense Motive DC 20 reveals that it alters the liar as well as the listener.

Prezzo aggiornato

500 silver

Uso consigliato

Debiti, contratti e garanzie sporche.

Descrizione superficiale

Anello spesso di bronzo e ferro, inciso con una bilancia. Pesa più di quanto dovrebbe e lascia un segno chiaro sulla pelle anche dopo pochi minuti.

Proprieta da identificato

Description

The ring serves as magical collateral. While worn, the creature holding the matching debt-token or written claim gains a +4 circumstance bonus on social checks made in the black market to prove that the wearer owes a debt or accepted an agreement.
